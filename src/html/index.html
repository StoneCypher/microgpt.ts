MicroGPT.ts

Karpathy's MicroGPT, but in TypeScript

What is this?

Karpathy wrote a single-file GPT in Python. I've converted it to TypeScript to bring it to a wider audience.

There are two versions:

  1. Single file, like Karpathy's
  2. Split into several files, with docblock and tests
